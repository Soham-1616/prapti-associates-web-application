About Us

Our Story

Building Dreams Since 2012

Prapti Associates was founded with a simple yet powerful mission — to deliver construction solutions that combine quality, innovation, and integrity. Over the past decade, we have grown from a small consultancy into a trusted name in the construction industry.

Our journey has been marked by a commitment to excellence, completing over 150 projects across residential, commercial, and institutional sectors. Every structure we build is a testament to our dedication to craftsmanship and client satisfaction.

Today, Prapti Associates stands as a full-service construction consultancy offering end-to-end solutions — from architectural planning and interior design to structural engineering and project management.
